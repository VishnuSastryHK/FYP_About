MONIQUA


An Autonomous Lake Water Monitoring using Real Time Analytics

About

The combined effects of increased population, pollution, and climate changes have drastically affected the water bodies around us. Lakes and ponds of any size are complex ecosystems with numerous inputs and outputs that can impact water quality in subtle or drastic ways.

A real-time monitoring system enables lake management professionals , environmentalists, and public to stay abreast of constantly changing water quality, and address potential issues before they develop into larger problems. We have developed an autonomous real-time lake water monitoring system.

The design and development of a low-cost surface vehicle as a tool to carry water sensors and transmit their results wirelessly was successfully implemented. In the future, the Model will be tested in a complicated environments to develop more robust and stable methodology, extra sensors and algorithms will be added to improve the performance of water quality monitoring.
